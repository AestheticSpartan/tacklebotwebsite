1. Overview

TackleBot is the ultimate fishing RPG for Discord. With over 15 unique locations, hundreds of fish species, and a dynamic economy, it offers an immersive experience right in your server. Whether you're a casual angler or a competitive tycoon, there's something for everyone.

Start your journey with a basic rod and work your way up to legendary equipment. Explore the depths of the ocean, mysterious cosmic seas, and everything in between.

2. Core Fishing Mechanics

Fishing

To start fishing, simply use the /fish cast command. You'll catch fish based on your current location and bait. Different biomes have different fish, so exploration is key.

Bait

Use specific baits to target legendary species or increase your catch rate. Baits can be bought in the shop or crafted.

3. Economy & Currency

Gold is the primary currency in TackleBot. Earn it by selling your catch at the market using /fish inventory. You can earn even more by processing fish via the Cove company.

Use Gold to buy upgrades, businesses, and consumables in the /fish shop and /fish company. Manage your finances wisely to become the wealthiest fisher in the server.

4. Business Empire

Once you've established yourself, you can start your own business empire. Buy Companies to earn passive income while you are away.

Invest in Several different routes to automate your income.

5. Pet System

Hatch pets from eggs found while fishing. Pets are loyal companions that provide huge boosts to your fishing stats.

Pet Bonuses

  • Catch Rate: Increases the chance of catching fish and how many you catch.
  • Sell Value: Increases the gold earned from selling fish.
  • Luck: Increases the chance of rare fish.

Collect them all and find the perfect companion for your playstyle!

6. Social & Competitive

Leaderboards

Compete in global leaderboards for the biggest catch, most gold, or highest level. Show off your achievements to the world.

Clans

Form fishing clans with your friends. Work together to unlock clan-wide bonuses and compete against other clans.

7. Progression Systems

Level up your fishing skill to unlock new areas and features. Each level brings new challenges and rewards.

Complete Achievements to earn badges and special boosts. The journey from a novice angler to a fishing master is filled with discoveries.
